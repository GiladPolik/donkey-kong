RESULT FILES FORMAT:

will include due of information of type: ( GameTime , result )
gametime will tell the time of the event, result will be one of the Enum variables in Class Result (finished, hitEnemy etc.)
the last due of information will be of type: ( score, result )
instead of time, well have the score at the end of the game.

STEPS FILES FORMAT:

will include due of information of type: ( GameTime, Key)
gametime will indicate as well for the time of the event, and Key will represent the key input of the user ( 'a' , 's' , 'd' , 'w' , 'p' )
